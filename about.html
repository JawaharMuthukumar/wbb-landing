Our Story

Built by friends.
Driven by
purpose.

WBB. — White Black Box is a software studio born from a simple question: what if we built software that actually matters?

We are a group of friends from Coimbatore, India — developers, designers, and builders — who decided to stop building things for others without purpose and start building with intention.

"Never build something
that doesn't matter."

WBB. started with a commitment — not a business plan. A group of friends who shared a belief: great software should be accessible to every founder, every business, every person with a real idea.

We were tired of seeing startups pay agency rates for average work. Tired of seeing good ideas die because founders couldn't find a reliable technical partner. So we became that partner.

We operate from Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu — one of India's growing tech hubs — and deliver to clients across India, the UK, the US, and globally. Our India base means we can offer world-class development at a fraction of the price of a Western agency.

The name — White Black Box — reflects our model perfectly. The White Side: products we build publicly under our own name. The Black Side: products we build for you, invisibly — no WBB branding, no portfolio mention, fully yours.

Every project we take on gets the same attention, the same care, and the same commitment to quality — whether it's a £299 landing page or a £30,000 SaaS platform.
